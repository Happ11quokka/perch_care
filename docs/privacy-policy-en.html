Perch Care Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 27, 2026

Perch Care (the "App") values your personal information and processes it as follows in compliance with applicable privacy laws.

1. Personal Information Collected

The App collects the following information to provide services:

  • Email address - Sign-up and login
  • Password - Account authentication (stored in encrypted form)
  • Nickname - User identification
  • Social login information - Identifier and email provided when linking Google, Apple, or Kakao accounts

2. Pet-Related Information

Pet information entered directly by users:

  • Pet name, species, breed, birth date, and sex
  • Weight records, diet records, and water intake records
  • Health check records and AI analysis results
  • Profile image

3. Automatically Collected Information

The following information may be collected automatically while using the App:

  • Device identifier (FCM token) - For push notifications (deleted immediately on logout or account deletion)
  • AI encyclopedia usage metadata - Question length, response time, and model information (original question text is not stored)
  • Access IP, access time, service usage history, and device information (OS, device model)

4. Purpose of Use

  • Member management and service provision
  • Pet health management features
  • AI-based health analysis services
  • Push notifications (health record reminders and service notices)
  • Analysis of usage metadata to improve AI encyclopedia quality
  • Service improvements and bug fixes

5. Retention and Deletion

When a user requests account deletion, all collected personal information is deleted without delay. During service use, information is retained only for service provision purposes.

6. Third-Party Disclosure

The App does not provide user personal information to third parties in principle, except in the following cases:

  • When the user has given prior consent
  • When required by law

7. External Services

The App uses the following external services:

AI health analysis and encyclopedia features are provided through a Perch-designed and operated AI model service.

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google) - Push notifications (device identifiers are transmitted)
  • Google Sign-In, Apple Sign In, Kakao Login - Social login features

8. User Rights

Users may exercise the following rights at any time:

  • Request access, correction, and deletion of personal information
  • Request account deletion
  • Request suspension of personal information processing
